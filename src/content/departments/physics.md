The Department of Physics encourages students to understand natural phenomena through observation, experimentation, and mathematical reasoning. Students learn core physics while developing the discipline required for scientific work.

## Learning focus

The department gives attention to laboratory practice, problem solving, and seminar presentations. Students are encouraged to connect classroom ideas with real-world systems.

## Facilities

- Undergraduate physics laboratory
- Demonstration equipment for mechanics, optics, and electricity
- Faculty-led study circles
- Science exhibition preparation
