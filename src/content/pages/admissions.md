Admissions for the 2026 academic year are open for undergraduate programs in science, commerce, and computer applications. Applicants should review eligibility, prepare required documents, and submit the application before the published deadline.

## Admission process

1. Choose a program from the departments section.
2. Complete the application form at the college office or online admission desk.
3. Submit academic records, identity proof, photographs, and category certificates if applicable.
4. Attend counselling or document verification as scheduled.
5. Pay the first-term fee to confirm admission.

## Documents required

- Class 10 and Class 12 mark sheets
- Transfer certificate
- Identity proof
- Passport-size photographs
- Caste or income certificate if applicable

## Need help?

The admissions office is open Monday to Friday from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Families can contact the office through the contact page for program guidance or fee details.
